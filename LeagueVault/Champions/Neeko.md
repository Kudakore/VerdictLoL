## Passive — Inherent Glamour

Neeko can look like an ally champion or as other units on the map. Receiving immobilizing crowd control, casting damaging spells, damaging enemy towers as a non-champion, or your disguise taking damage equal to its healthbar breaks the disguise.

## Abilities

### Q — Blooming Burst

Neeko throws a seed dealing magic damage. The seed blooms again on hitting champions or killing units.

**Cooldown:** 9/8.5/8/7.5/7s  **Cost:** 50/60/70/80/90  **Range:** 800

### W — Shapesplitter

Neeko passively deals bonus magic damage every third attack, briefly increasing her move speed. Neeko can activate to send a clone in a direction and recast to change the direction of the clone.

**Cooldown:** 16/15/14/13/12s  **Range:** 900

### E — Tangle-Barbs

Neeko slings a tangle that damages and roots everything it passes through. If the tangle kills an enemy or passes through a champion, it becomes larger, faster, and roots for longer.

**Cooldown:** 12/11.5/11/10.5/10s  **Cost:** 60/65/70/75/80  **Range:** 1000

### R — Pop Blossom

After a short preparation, Neeko leaps into the air, knocking up all nearby enemies. Upon landing, nearby enemies are damaged and stunned. The preparation is hidden if Neeko is disguised.

**Cooldown:** 120/105/90s  **Cost:** 100  **Range:** 600

## Lore

Hailing from a long lost tribe of vastaya, Neeko can blend into any crowd by borrowing the appearances of others, even absorbing something of their emotional state to tell friend from foe in an instant. No one is ever sure where—or who—Neeko might be, but those who intend to do her harm will soon witness her true colors revealed, and feel the full power of her primordial spirit magic unleashed upon them.
